THE BROTHER

A Leaving Cert points maximiser. Find the biggest leak first. Fix it. Move on.

What this does

Results Coach reads pasted exam or class-test results, identifies the biggest points leak, explains why it matters, and sends the student into a focused Gym block.

Practice Mode generates short authentic Leaving Cert style questions on the mechanics: formulae, definitions, quotes, keywords, short structured responses, grammar, and other small bits that leak marks.

Practice scores are stored and fed into the running picture too, but with a lighter weight than full exam-result inputs.
